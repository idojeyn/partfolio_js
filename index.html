About Us

Flowers are the most beautiful and colorful parts of a plant. They are used for reproduction in flowering plants. Most flowers have petals, sepals, stamens, and a pistil. Flowers attract insects and birds with their colors and smell. This helps with pollination. After pollination, flowers produce seeds and fruits. There are two main types of flowers: Complete flowers and incomplete flowers. A complete flower has all four parts: Sepals, petals, stamens, and pistil. Examples include roses, lilies, and tulips. An incomplete flower is missing one or more parts. For example, corn has flowers without petals. Flowers can also be unisexual or bisexual. A unisexual flower has either stamens or pistils. A bisexual flower has both stamens and pistils. Flowers are very important in nature and human life. They are used in medicine, decoration, perfumes, and food.
